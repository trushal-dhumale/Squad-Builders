UX Researcher

Company: Spotify

Location: Stockholm, Sweden

Salary: $85k - $110k

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description

At Spotify, we're looking for an experienced UX Researcher to join our design team and help us better understand our users. You will help uncover deep insights about how people use Spotify, what their needs are, and how we can create better, more engaging audio experiences.

Responsibilities
  • Design and conduct qualitative and quantitative research to inform product decisions.
  • Work closely with product managers, designers, and engineers to identify research needs and opportunities.
  • Analyze data and translate insights into actionable recommendations.
  • Communicate findings in a clear and compelling way to stakeholders.
Qualifications
  • 2+ years of UX research experience in a product-focused company.
  • Experience with a variety of research methods including usability testing, interviews, and surveys.
  • Strong analytical, communication, and storytelling skills.
  • Bachelor's or Master's in HCI, Psychology, Human Factors, or related field.
