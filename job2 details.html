Product Designer

Company: Slack

Location: Remote

Salary: $90k - $120k

Job Type: Full-time

Job Description

Slack is looking for a talented and creative Product Designer to help shape the future of how teams communicate. You will collaborate with product managers, developers, and researchers to design intuitive and delightful experiences that solve real user problems.

Responsibilities
  • Design and prototype user experiences for new features and improvements.
  • Conduct user research and usability testing.
  • Work closely with product and engineering teams in an agile environment.
  • Maintain design consistency across the product suite.
Qualifications
  • 3+ years of experience in product/UI/UX design.
  • Proficiency with design tools like Figma, Sketch, or Adobe XD.
  • Strong portfolio showcasing product design work.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
